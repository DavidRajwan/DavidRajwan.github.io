About

As a passionate computer engineer with a focus on chip-scale integration, robotics autonomy and controls, and AI/machine learning, I thrive in environments that push the boundaries of software and hardware design. My experiences range from leading the design and manufacturing of an infantry robot at Virginia Tech's Robo-Grinder Club to building lightsabers from scratch as part of the Lightsaber Club.

Professionally, I've developed control software for Altec's utility truck fleet, designed a universal communication protocol for servo-driven systems at Moog, and verified Verilog software using UVM while establishing MIL-STD-1553 data bus requirements at Boeing. I'm eager to connect and explore new opportunities in embedded systems, robotics, or chip design!
